How Fixer Works

  1. Scans running processes and foreground activity.
  2. Chooses the optimal profile for current user context.
  3. Applies priority and policy actions with safety guardrails.
  4. Writes logs and optional learning suggestions for tuning.

Safety Modes

Safe: observe and log only.

Balanced: throttle suspicious or wasteful tasks.

Aggressive: can terminate confirmed high-risk or abusive tasks.
